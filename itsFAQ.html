Finding your Network ID

Please refer to the steps below in order to lookup your Network ID!

How to Find your Network ID

1.  Please first visit my.gannon.edu

2.  After getting redirected to the website, you should now scroll down to the "What's My Network ID" link

3.  Once you have found the link you must fill out the form provided. It will require you to enter your last name and either one of the following: Gannon ID, Social Security Number, or SEVIS ID (Only for International Students).

4.  Press submit.

Your Network ID should now be available to you.

What is My Default Password?

Forgetting your password is something that many of us experience, and it can be rather annoying. Luckily Gannon ITS has a plan for you if you forget!

Your Default Password

Your default password is the last four numbers of your Social Security Number followed by the capitla initials of your first and last name.

NOTE: If you are an international student, this will be your SEVIS ID instead.

How do I connect to Gannon's Wireless Network?

Connecting to Gannon's wireless network can sometimes be a bit difficult if you are not toally aware with all of the prerequisites that need to happen before you are able to connect to the network on your mobile device or computer.

Helpful Tips to Connect to the Internet

1.  Ensure your device is not in airplane mode and your Wifi is turned on.

2.  If you are on Gannon's campus, select "GannonWireless".

3.  Open the internet browser of your choice on your devide, if you are not able to search the internet you should be redirected to the registration site. If not, pleace access netreg.gannon.edu

4.  Follow the steps as prompted by the network registration site. At the end you will be required to go through the wireless scan which requires downloading the Bradford Dissolvable Agent client, if successful you will be redirected to the internet. If not please continue reading.

5.  Below is a list of things you should try before visiting ITS to diagnose the issue on your PC.

  • In order to pass the scan successfully, you must have some type of antivirus installed on your computer. If you have a Windows computer, then you will alreay have Windows Defender which is what Gannon reccomends. However, if you have a Mac, then Gannon reccomends the Avira® software and it is free.
  • Make sure your antivirus software is up to date.
  • Ensure that your operating system is up to date.
  • Restart your computer.

Please note that you are due for a network scan every thirty days. If there are still problems, then you should consider reaching out to Gannon's ITS Department and schedule an appointment.

What is My Email Address?

Your email address is what keeps you connected to everything that is going on here at campus and it is important to remember what it is!

Your Default Password

Your email address is simply "networkID@knights.gannon.edu".

Getting your Email In Sync with your Phone (iPhone)
  1. On your device go to Settings > Mail > Contacts > Calendars
  2. Select "Add Account"
  3. Select "Microsoft Exchange"
  4. Enter your full email address in the "Email" field
  5. Skip the domain
  6. Enter your full email address for the "Username" field and your password in the "Password" field.
  7. Select Next.
  8. Verify that the server name is outlook.office365.com
  9. Select Next.

FURTHER: If you do not have the Microsoft Outlook app already downloaded on your phone, download it from the Google Play Store or Apple's App Store. This will give you the option to be notified on the go when you receieve important emails. This is not a bad option if you have more than one email you have to manage!

Registering your Personal Device to Gannon's Wireless Network

Please refer to the steps below in order to get your personal device such as an Xbox or Apple TV connected!

Steps to Register Personal Device

1.  Please first locate the MAC address on your device. If you are having trouble locating this on your device, please refer to this link to help locate it! Find your MAC Address

2.  With your MAC address in mind, visit this website on a computer that is connected to Gannon's network already Register Device

3.  On this site please enter your network ID, password, device type, and MAC address.

4.  Press submit.

Your device should now be connected to the internet. If you are still experiencing problems, please send the help desk a picture of your MAC address at helpdesk@gannon.edu

FURTHER: If you are trying to connect your device via the ethernet jacks available in the residence halls, please keep in mind these are shut off by default. If you would like to reactivate them, please call the Help Desk and schdule a visit. Also keep in mind that this will be a low priority issue and may take some time.

Downloading and Installing Office 365 for Free

Please refer to the steps below in order to get your free subscription of Office 365

Steps to Register Personal Device

1.  Please visit this website www.office.com/getoffice365

2.  Sign up using your Gannon email address, an email will be sent shortly after supplying this information.

3.  Check your Gannon email. Follow the instructions sent in that email and the download will begin. Please keep in mind that this is a relatively large file and may take some time to download and install.
