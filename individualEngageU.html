Account Settings

Ways to Search

1.  In order to manage your account settings you will have to sign in first. In the upper right hand corner of the home page you will notice a large blue button labled, "SIGN IN". Click that button.

2.  You will now be prompted to enter your network credentials, these will be the same credentials you use for Office 365, Blackboard, MyGannon, etc. After this has been done, click the green "Sign In" button.

3.  After being redirected to the home page you will now notice a large circular button in the upper right hand corner with the initial of your first name. Once that is clicked, click the first option that shows up in the navigation bar.

On this page you will be able to manage your profile, privacy settings, how you receive notifications, and interests. By setting these up properly, you will certainly enrich your experience you have on engageU.

Search Organizations

Searching for organizations is made very easy with EngageU. It can be a bit overwhelming with all of the clubs and organizations offered at Gannon. Luckily with the tools shown in this feature, you will learn how to accomplish this fast and effectively.

Ways to Search

1.  After logging in, you will notice a large search bar in the middle of the home page. This can be very effective if you already know the club or organization you are looking for.

2.  This method is much more useful when you do not know exactly what clubs and organizations are out there. By following the steps below you can get to the page.

1. Sign in with your Gannon Network ID and Passowrd if not done already

2. On the main navigation bar, click "ORGANIZATIONS"

On this page you can find an organization that suits your interest very easily! You can now filter down organizations that suit your interests and see the results on the right hand side of the page.

Contacting an Organization

Contacting an organization is easy to achieve on EngageU. By contacting an organization at Gannon you can accomplish many things wheter it be stating your interest in them, reporting a problem you have with them, getting information about an upcoming event, and much more!

How to Contact an Organization

1.  After logging in, look for the "ORGANIZATIONS" tab in the upper left hand corner of the navigation bar.

2.  After getting redirected to the page, you can now either filter the organizations by using the Category radio buttons or searching the organization directly.

3.  Once you have found the organization you are looking for, go ahead and click on it. Once redirected to that organization's page click on the "CONTACT" button.

4.  Now, fill out the form and be sure to include your Name, Email Address, and Subject. For the Subject dropdown there are many options available including, "Interested in Getting Involved, Inquiring About an Upcoming Event, Reporting a Concern, Media or Publicity Inquiry, Oppurtunity to Collaborate, and Other".

5.  When you are satisfied with your mesage, you can then submit the form.

All you have to do now is be patient and a representative from that organization should be in contact with you shortly!

Searching for Events

One of the largest used features on EngageU is finding interesting upcoming events on campus. Luckily, EngageU provides powerful functionality to help you narrow down on events that you would like to attend.

Ways to Search

1.  After logging in, you will notice a large search bar in the middle of the home page. This can be very effective if you already know the event you are looking for and just wish to obtain more details about it.

2.  When you are trying to find events that interest you and don't know where to start, then clicking the "Events" tab in the upper left hand side of the navigation bar can help you achieve that. With the steps below, you can help narrow down events that cater to your interests.

1. Sign in with your Gannon Network ID and Passowrd if not done already

2. On the main navigation bar, click "ORGANIZATIONS"

On this page you can find an organization that suits your interest very easily! You can now filter down organizations that suit your interests and see the results on the right hand side of the page.

Starting a Discussion

Starting discussion on a recently upcoming news article uploaded can be a powerful way to clarify any misunderstandings, voice your opinion on subject matter, or even reach out to people who have similar opinions to you on a news article!

How to Start a Discussion on News Article

1.  If you are not logged in, you will first need to do so by clicking the blue "Sign In" button in the top right hand corner of the website. You will be unable to start a discussion without logging in.

2.  Once logged in, on the main navigation bar you will see a tab labled "News". Click on that link.

3.  On this page you will notice a list of recent news atricles in which you will be able to click into.

4.  After exploring the news article that you are interesting in starting a discussion on, you will be able to do so at the bottom of the page.

Once you are satisfied with your discussion thread post, other people will be able to see it and react to it!
