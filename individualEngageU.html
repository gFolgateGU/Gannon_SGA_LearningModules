Search Organizations

Searching for organizations is made very easy with EngageU. It can be a bit overwhelming with all of the clubs and organizations offered at Gannon. Luckily with the tools shown in this feature, you will learn how to accomplish this fast and effectively.

Ways to Search

1.  After logging in, you will notice a large search bar in the middle of the home page. This can be very effective if you already know the club or organization you are looking for.

2.  This method is much more useful when you do not know exactly what clubs and organizations are out there. By following the steps below you can get to the page to do so

1. Sign in with your Gannon Network ID and Passowrd if not done already

2. On the main navigation bar, click "ORGANIZATIONS"

On this page you can find an organization that suits your interest very easily! You can now filter down organizations that suit your interests and see the results on the right hand side of the page.
