Managing an Club or Organization with EngageU

Learn what it takes to keep your club or organization running smoothly

This Module's Goal

Increased Organization Efficiency

Managing a club or organization can be a daunting task, especially as it continues to grow. With EngageU you can effectively manage your club and help ensure that it runs smoothly. With these helpful tools EngageU offers, you can remain focused on achieving your goals and worry less about maintaining your club or organization.

Start Learning!

Select an option from the dropdown above, to begin!

Getting to the Action Center

1.  If you have not done so already log in in the upper right hand corner by supplying your Gannon network credentials.

2.  Now, in the right hand corner of the home page next to your initial click the grid button.

3.  There will be two options listed (Explore and Manage). Click manage.

You will now be at the action center where you can perform administrative tasks on your club or organization!

Managing your Organization's Roster

Getting to the Roster Page

1.  In the Action Center at the top of the page, there will be a list of the organizations that you belong to. Select the organization that you want to manage the roster for.

2.  Once you are there in the upper left hand corner of the page you will notice an icon with three rows on it and the name of your organization. Click that icon.

3.  In that menu select "Roster"

How to Manage the Roster

Above the table with all of your members you will notice three options.

  • "Current" is the default option and it allows you to remove and edit current members in the club or organization. On this tab you will also be able to change the roles of your current members.
  • "Pending" contains a list of people awaiting to accept your invitation to join the club or organization. On this tab you also have the ability to resend the invitation or delete it.
  • "Prospective" contains the list of people who want to join your organization. On this tab you have the ability to approve or deny the requests.

2.  Once you are there in the upper left hand corner of the page you will notice an icon with three rows on it and the name of your organization. Click that icon.

Manage your Documents

2.  Once you are there in the upper left hand corner of the page you will notice an icon with three rows on it and the name of your organization. Click that icon.
